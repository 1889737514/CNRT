- xx RS: Beijing (Beijing RS), Shanghai (Shanghai RS), Guangzhou (Guangzhou RS), etc.
- RS: Changsha

- Tranditional name of the area: Chongqing (Lianglukou), Shenzhen (Luohu), Chengdu (North RS), Hangzhou (Chengzhan)
